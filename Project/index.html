Java Junction Café

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase the menu, location, and events at Java Junction Café, and to allow customers to place orders online.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What are the hours of operation, and where is the café located?
  • Scenario 2: Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options on your menu?

Color Schema

The selected colors for this site are:

  • Primary Color: #4A90E2 (Used for headings and buttons)
  • Secondary Color: #F5A623 (Used for accents and highlights)

Typography

The selected fonts for this site are:

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif
  • Body Text: 'Lato', sans-serif
